Welcome to Max's Ice House

We're glad you're here. Since 2018, Mas has been my dog. He's a really good boy, and he happens to cook really good food. Fan favorites include corned beef with a potatoe kale hash, blueberry nutmeg pancakes, and a sweet and savory duck in a honey braising sauce. Max also makes a mean mint mojito, and his house-aged scotch brings you back to 1776.

Max is awake from 6AM to 10 PM, and during random intervals during the night. Stop on bye his kitchen anytime for a tasty bite!
